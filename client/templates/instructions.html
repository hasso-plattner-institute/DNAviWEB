DNAvi - Cell-free DNA visualization and analysis

1. How it works

Step 1: upload gel electrophoresis image or signal table.

Step 2: choose your DNA size standard

Step 3: (optional) Add sample metadata

Need help? Use our documentation.

2. Submit for Analysis

Your DNA file will be analysed for peaks, height normalized, and grouped if metadata is provided. Be patient, this may take a while. If logged in, you can also choose to save your analysis files for future use in the submissions dashboard.

3. Explore Analysis Results & Download

View your analysis results interactively in the web interface, or download a ZIP folder containing all results and plots. You are allowed to use this for your work only if you cite DNAvi.
